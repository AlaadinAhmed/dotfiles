#Aladdin's Dotfiles
I created these dotfiles hoping to help my friends, and anyone who wishes to get in action without the overhead of configuring every minute details on their system.. I hope to grow this repo as I use many different tools, and by the community requests, as possible.
## Content
The Dotfiles contains
- Hyprland Configs
- Quickshell ( It's a modular widget-based way to setup your desktup environment... it's the best way to rice your environment without having to install a ton of packages).
- Neovim
- Ghossty ( A feature-rich terminal emulator, that supports GPU acceleration).
